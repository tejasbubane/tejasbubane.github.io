Who are you, and what do you do?

I am a programmer with close to 7 years of experience. I build websites & APIs with Ruby on Rails & ReactJS during my dayjob. During my spare time, I play around with different programming languages like Haskell, Clojure, Elixir, ML, etc.

What hardware do you use?

My primary computer is Macbook Pro 13 inch 2017 model. It is lightweight and easy to carry around. The butterfly keys are not too good, especially because I was using a cherry MX blue keyboard before, but I am used to this since past few months. But I sure miss the travel and sweet clicks of the cherries.

I am not a heavy phone user - whatsapp & twitter are my primary apps. So I generally prefer mid-range android phones so I don’t need to worry about damaging or losing it. I currently own a Samsung M30 with 3GB RAM & Android 10.

Headphones are Sennheiser CX 275 S. I prefer in-ear since they are light & easy to carry around.

And what software?

Emacs user since almost 8 years right from my college days - I am a huge fan. My emacs config is here. Heavy user of magit for all things git - this is hands-down the best git tool out there. I switched to Firefox as my primary browser after its quantum release and never looked back. For window management I used spectacle for a long time, but recently found out it is not maintained anymore so switched to rectangle. Terminal is iTerm 2 with zsh using my custom config. For video calls I use Zoom or Skype.

What would be your dream setup?

I find my current setup sufficient. My dream setup would be a nice 4k monitor with an ergonomic mechanical keyboard like ergodox or atreus 2.
